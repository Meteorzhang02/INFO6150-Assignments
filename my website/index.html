Precautions for purchasing a second-hand car

  • Check the vehicle's history and maintenance status
  • Verify the vehicle identification code (VIN)
  • Have a comprehensive test drive experience
  • Check the vehicle body for any repair marks
  • Understand the market price to avoid overbidding
